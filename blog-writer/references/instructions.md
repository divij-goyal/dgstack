# Blog Writer Instructions

First classify the blog.

Common blog types:

- Founder essay
- Product launch
- Opinion piece
- Explainer
- Tactical how-to
- Personal story
- Industry analysis

Then choose a fitting style model.

Examples:

| Style model | Use when |
| --- | --- |
| Paul Graham | Founder essays, startup insight, contrarian but plain thinking |
| George Orwell | Clear argument, moral clarity, plain English, no fluff |
| Lenny's Newsletter | Product/growth writing, tactical frameworks, operator audience |
| Ben Thompson | Strategy, aggregation theory, business model analysis |
| Packy McCormick | Energetic tech narrative, market excitement, company storytelling |
| Morgan Housel | Human behavior, money, timeless lessons, story-led reflection |
| Stratechery-style memo | Market structure, platform shifts, strategic clarity |

Do not imitate living authors line-by-line. Use the fit: structure, clarity,
pacing, level of detail, and reader promise.
