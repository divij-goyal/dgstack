# Tagline Generator Instructions

Use this skill for concise startup positioning.

Good lines usually do one of these:

- Name the customer and job
- Make a complex product instantly legible
- Claim a new category
- State the outcome, not the feature list
- Compress the company into a memorable phrase

Examples from YC-style startup descriptions:

| Company | Line |
| --- | --- |
| Hyper | Company brain that powers your AI employees |
| Superset | The IDE for the AI Agents Era |
| Apollo Atomics | We make the most compact nuclear reactors |
| TesterArmy | Test your app with AI, catch bugs before users do |
| Tasklet | Agents that own the work |
| Foaster | The AI-native McKinsey |
| ReasonBlocks | The runtime layer that makes AI agents cheaper and more reliable |

Avoid:

- "Revolutionizing..."
- "AI-powered platform for..."
- "Unlock your potential..."
- Any line that could describe 100 other startups
