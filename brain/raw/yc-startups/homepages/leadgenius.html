What is Precision Data?

Precision Data is highly specific and verified datasets tailored to a business’s unique needs, rather than generic, one-size-fits-all records.

How is LeadGenius different?

Unlike static databases, LeadGenius delivers on-demand data, AI + Human-in-the-Loop sourcing, and global coverage across hard-to-find segments—fully compliant with GDPR and CCPA.

Pricing depends on your needs and scale. Packages are tailored to business size and requirements. Contact LeadGenius for a detailed quote.

LeadGenius leverages AI and LLMs to process billions of resources, combined with Human-in-the-Loop checks for accuracy and strategic depth. The Tagger system also organizes insights and creates lookalike audiences.
