An agent for every opportunity

Each agent owns a focused job. Activate what you need now, add more later. Or connect Vector to your own LLM via Volantio's MCP server.

Revenue Optimization

Shifts demand from constrained flights to lower-demand alternatives, capturing revenue that would otherwise spill.

Overbooking

Overbooking can be driven by revenue optimization or operational requirements. Vector makes it customer friendly by proactively moving the right passengers before departure, so no one gets to the gate without a seat.

Operational Disruption

Flight disruptions happen every day. Vector helps resolve them proactively by identifying the right passengers to move before departure, reducing complexity and delivering a better experience for passengers.

Demand Intelligence

Demand does not start with a booking. Vector identifies where customers want to travel, how prices influence decisions, and where demand is building long before it reaches your reservations system.
