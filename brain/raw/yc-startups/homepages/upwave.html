The Brand Outcomes Measurement Platform

Upwave brings science to top-of-funnel–measuring Brand Lift, validating Brand Reach, and surfacing Brand Optimization opportunities in one, dynamic platform. With cross-channel brand measurement for CTV, Digital, Social, Linear, Addressable, Retail Media, Streaming Audio and more, Upwave maximizes the effectiveness of brand spend.
Learn More
WATCH VIDEO

Dynamic, Real-Time Dashboard to Maximize Brand Lift

With Upwave’s real-time platform, marketers can create dynamic campaign dashboards, access all modules, and easily see if the campaign is driving lift.

Cross-Channel Coverage

Cross-channel measurement with accurate, privacy-safe data in a unified dashboard for CTV, Digital, Social, Linear, Addressable, Retail Media, and Streaming Audio.

Granular Cut-Level Controls

Evaluate Brand Lift at a tactical level, giving granular insights to compare performance of media, creative and more.

Better Math and Methodology

Upwave’s methodology is designed, and meticulously checked, to ensure our sample is high-quality, census-representative and clean of bias or other interference.

Put a Performance Lens on Brand Investment

Upwave allows marketers to regularly measure, improve, and maximize with an always-on, analytical rigor for brand campaigns.

AI-Powered Insights and Reports

Bayes, Upwave's AI Agent, helps customers turn data into digestible, on-demand insights both in-platform and available to download–at the click of a button.

Actionable Optimization Recommendations

Upwave’s Brand Optimization is purpose-built as a tool for decision makers to clearly understand over–and underperforming–
tactics, without a statistician on staff.

On-Target Profiles and Reach

Upwave uses real attitudinal data and machine learning to verify whether a campaign is reaching your brand-specific audience–
beyond age and gender.
