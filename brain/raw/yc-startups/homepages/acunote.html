How Acunote Is Different

1. Fast and Easy to Use

Acunote is fast and easy to use for everybody. Small teams can keep their todo lists. Large enterprises can track the work of hundreds of employees.

Acunote is fast no matter how much data you have and usable for both technical and non-technical users.

2. Shows Actual Progress

Because Acunote is fast and easy, people use it for their daily work. This way actual execution data gets into the system.

Using that data Acunote shows you the actual progress in burndown charts, not just wishful thinking.

3. Provides Powerful Analytics

Acunote lets you roll up the execution data into reports appropriate for any level of corporate hierarchy - from workers to CEO.

This way you can manage individuals, teams and the whole company in a data-driven fashion.

4. Works for the Whole Company

Acunote works for the whole company as a unified project management, communication and collaboration tool. It is a single place for you to see the status of your company's efforts.

5. Integrates with Existing Tools

Acunote integrates with tools that you may already use: Google Apps, source control systems (Subversion, GitHub, Bitbucket, Perforce), bugtrackers (JIRA, Trac, FogBugz, Bugzilla, Mantis).
