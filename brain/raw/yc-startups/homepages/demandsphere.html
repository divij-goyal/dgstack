FAQ

Frequently asked questions

DemandSphere tracks your brand's visibility across three search surfaces: traditional SERPs (rankings, SERP features, AI Overviews, AI Mode), AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot - mentions, citations, and sentiment), and server logs (crawl behavior from Googlebot, AI bots, and other crawlers). All of this data flows into a unified BigQuery data warehouse you can query directly.

Most SEO platforms track SERP rankings. Some have started adding basic AI search features. DemandSphere is the only platform that unifies SERP analytics, AI search visibility tracking, server log analytics, and a BigQuery data warehouse in a single product. We also track at significantly larger scale - 200+ markets, 10+ search engines, and millions of prompts across AI search engines daily.

We track ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, Google AI Overviews, Google AI Mode, and more. For each platform, we monitor whether your brand is mentioned, cited with a link, or absent, and how that changes over time. We were the first platform to track Google AI Mode within 24 hours of its launch.

The platform is modular. Most teams start with DemandMetrics for SERP analytics or DemandMetrics for Gen AI for AI search visibility, then expand to log analytics or the data warehouse as their program matures. You can add products at any time without migrating data.

All DemandSphere data (SERP rankings, AI search mentions, log analytics, GSC, and GA4) flows directly into BigQuery tables in your own Google Cloud project. You can query with SQL, connect Data Studio (formerly Looker Studio) or Tableau, build custom models, or export to Snowflake. No ETL pipeline to build or maintain.

It's a 30-minute call where we run DemandSphere on your actual domain. You'll see your current SERP positions, AI visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and more, and how your brand appears (or doesn't) in AI-generated answers. All with your real data. No slides, no prep required on your end.

Most teams are fully onboarded within a week. SERP tracking starts producing data within 24 hours. AI search visibility monitoring begins immediately. If you add Search Intelligence, your BigQuery tables are provisioned as part of that setup. We handle the configuration - you just provide your keywords and domains.

Plans start at $79/month and scale based on tracking volume - the number of SERP keywords and AI prompts you monitor. All plans include unlimited users, all search engines, and both SERP and AI visibility tracking. Enterprise plans add BigQuery, log analytics, SSO, and managed services.
