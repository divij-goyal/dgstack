Upload PCB files

Drag and drop your PCB files into the browser. Compatible with Altium, EAGLE, and KiCAD. You can also upload a BOM .csv/.tsv with your EDA files.

Reconcile BOM

Our system extracts part numbers from your EDA files and adds them to your BOM, alerting you to out-of-stock parts and MOQs.

Virtual Build

CircuitHub's board viewer previews your board and placements, helping you spot any mistakes before ordering.

Quote & Order

Select the number of assembled boards and your desired lead time. The real-time quote includes pricing for bare boards, parts, and assembly.

Monitor Order Progress

Our order page lets you track your order, view the estimated ship date, and communicate directly with the factory to resolve any issues.
