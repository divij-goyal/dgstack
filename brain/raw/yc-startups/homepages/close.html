What is Close?

Close is a sales CRM built for teams that sell. It combines calling, email, SMS, pipeline management, reporting, and an AI sales agent named Chloe into a single platform. Close helps teams act on their sales data.

What is Chloe?

Chloe is an AI sales agent built into Close CRM that calls leads, qualifies prospects through real conversations, and can book meetings while updating your CRM automatically. She is part of the CRM — not a separate tool or integration.
