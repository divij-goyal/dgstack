Frequency asked questions

What is Zentail?

Zentail is a multichannel platform that helps ecommerce brands manage and grow across online marketplaces without all the busywork. It automates how your products are listed, updated, and maintained across channels so you can get products live faster and keep everything accurate as marketplaces change.

How does Zentail help with marketplace listings?

Zentail takes the work out of listing products on multiple marketplaces. Instead of manually formatting and updating listings for each channel, it automatically structures your product data and pushes it out in the right format – so your listings are accurate, consistent, and ready to sell everywhere.

What is automated change management in ecommerce selling?

Automated change management means you don’t have to constantly fix your listings when marketplaces update their requirements. Zentail automatically adjusts your product data to stay compliant, so your listings stay live and optimized without ongoing manual updates.

Who is Zentail built for? 

Zentail is designed for ecommerce brands and sellers who want to grow across marketplaces without increasing operational complexity.It’s especially valuable for teams dealing with manual processes, inconsistent listings, low publish rates, or difficulty scaling beyond a single channel.

How does Zentail help increase sales on marketplaces?

Zentail helps you make more from marketplaces by getting more of your products live and keeping them optimized. When your listings are accurate, complete, and consistently maintained, they perform better. You get more visibility, higher conversions, and fewer missed sales.

How is Zentail different from other multichannel ecommerce tools?

Most listing tools require templates, manual mapping, and constant updates. Zentail removes that work. With SMART Types, you list your products once and publish everywhere with no reformatting required.As marketplaces change, Zentail automatically updates your listings to stay compliant, so you can keep products live and selling without ongoing maintenance.

How much does Zentail cost?

Zentail pricing starts at $300/month for smaller sellers and $2,000/month for established multichannel brands. Pricing is usage-based and scales with your catalog size, sales volume, and marketplace activity. For larger businesses with more complex needs, enterprise plans are also available. You can visit our pricing page or talk to our team to get a tailored quote.
